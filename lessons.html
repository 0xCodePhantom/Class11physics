Class 11 Physics Syllabus

Units & Chapters

Unit I: Physical World and Measurement

  • Units and Measurements
    • Need for measurement
    • Systems of units; SI units
    • Fundamental and derived units
    • Significant figures
    • Dimensions of physical quantities
    • Dimensional analysis and its applications

Unit II: Kinematics

  • Motion in a Straight Line
    • Frame of reference
    • Uniform & non-uniform motion
    • Instantaneous velocity
    • Velocity‑time and position‑time graphs
    • Elementary differentiation & integration for motion
  • Motion in a Plane
    • Scalars & vectors
    • Position and displacement vectors
    • Vector addition & multiplication
    • Unit vectors
    • Resolution of vectors in two dimensions
    • Projectile motion
    • Uniform circular motion

Unit III: Laws of Motion

  • Laws of Motion
    • Concept of force & inertia
    • Newton’s first, second, and third laws
    • Momentum and impulse
    • Conservation of linear momentum
    • Equilibrium of concurrent forces
    • Friction: static, kinetic, rolling
    • Lubrication
    • Centripetal force and circular motion

Unit IV: Work, Energy & Power

  • Work, Energy and Power
    • Work done by constant & variable force
    • Kinetic energy & work–energy theorem
    • Potential energy
    • Conservative vs non-conservative forces
    • Motion in a vertical circle
    • Elastic & inelastic collisions (1D & 2D)

Unit V: Motion of System of Particles and Rotational Motion

  • System of Particles & Rotational Motion
    • Centre of mass (two-particle system & rigid body)
    • Momentum conservation & centre of mass motion
    • Torque & moment of force
    • Angular momentum & its conservation
    • Rigid body rotation & equations of motion
    • Moment of inertia & radius of gyration

Unit VI: Gravitation

  • Gravitation
    • Kepler’s laws
    • Universal law of gravitation
    • Acceleration due to gravity (variation)
    • Gravitational potential & potential energy
    • Escape velocity
    • Orbital velocity of a satellite

Unit VII: Properties of Bulk Matter

  • Mechanical Properties of Solids
    • Elasticity, stress-strain relation
    • Hooke’s Law
    • Young’s, bulk & shear modulus
    • Poisson’s ratio
    • Elastic potential energy
  • Mechanical Properties of Fluids
    • Pressure in fluids; Pascal’s law
    • Viscosity & Stokes’ law
    • Terminal velocity
    • Streamline vs turbulent flow
    • Bernoulli’s theorem
    • Surface tension & capillarity
  • Thermal Properties of Matter
    • Heat & temperature
    • Thermal expansion of solids, liquids, gases
    • Specific heat; calorimetry
    • Latent heat
    • Heat transfer: conduction, convection, radiation

Unit VIII: Thermodynamics

  • Thermodynamics
    • Thermal equilibrium and temperature
    • Heat, work & internal energy
    • First law of thermodynamics
    • Second law: reversible and irreversible processes
    • Isothermal & adiabatic processes

Unit IX: Behaviour of Perfect Gases & Kinetic Theory

  • Kinetic Theory of Gases
    • Equation of state of a perfect gas
    • Work done in compressing a gas
    • Assumptions of kinetic theory
    • Pressure, rms speed
    • Degrees of freedom & energy equipartition
    • Mean free path & Avogadro’s number

Unit X: Oscillations & Waves

  • Oscillations
    • Periodic motion: period, frequency
    • Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM)
    • Phase, energy in SHM
    • Pendulum & restoring force
  • Waves
    • Transverse & longitudinal waves
    • Speed of wave, wave equation
    • Principle of superposition
    • Standing waves, harmonics
    • Beats
